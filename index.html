A curious UX designer fueled by coffee and collaboration.

Hi, I'm Rachel!

I'm a dreamer, creator, & designer. After working in the service industry for 10 years, I decided to pack up my skills of empathy and understanding and explore the world of design.

I'm passionate about environmentalism, learning, and empowering people to be the best version of themselves. I love walking down the street and seeing the influence of design in small, everyday interactions. I daydream about working on projects that make those interactions as user-centric and emotionally-driven as possible. I strongly believe the best way to take big, world changing steps is to start with small, impactful ideas.

I have an unusually strong love for coffee, music, and plants. In my spare time, you can find me creating a new story arc for my character in Dungeons and Dragons, hunting for a good local food spot to try (suggestions welcome), and playing fetch with my cat, Sushi.
